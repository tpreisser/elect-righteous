## Notable Public Messaging

- The official county page says the office goal is to provide quality public service to all citizens in a professional manner. Source: [Ellis County Register of Deeds](https://www.ellisco.net/100/Register-of-Deeds).
- The same page says the office files and records documents that pertain to real estate. Source: [Ellis County Register of Deeds](https://www.ellisco.net/100/Register-of-Deeds).
- The county page lists the state-required functions as recording qualified public documents and maintaining accurate indexes showing correct land ownership in the county. Source: [Ellis County Register of Deeds](https://www.ellisco.net/100/Register-of-Deeds).
- The official page says register of deeds records are required by law to be open to the public, with an exception for the Kansas Real Estate Sales Validation Questionnaire. Source: [Ellis County Register of Deeds](https://www.ellisco.net/100/Register-of-Deeds).
- The 2025 budget book adds that the office serves the public by recording and preserving county records, and says some sensitive records require requester forms before copies are given. Source: [Ellis County 2025 departmental budget book](https://www.ellisco.net/DocumentCenter/View/5647/2025-Budget-Book_Departmental-Budgets).
