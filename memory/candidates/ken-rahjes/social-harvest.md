## Public Platform Signals

Rahjes' campaign site is the central candidate-controlled channel. The homepage introduces him as a farm-and-ranch Kansan from Agra, says he lives there with his wife Lori, and connects his public identity to trust, integrity, rural Kansas, small business, agriculture communications, and legislative service. The site's current slogan block reads "TRUST...BUT VERIFY" and "Securing Your Future with Trust and Integrity." ([source](https://kenforkansas.com/))

The campaign site's own social block says, "Receive future updates from Ken on Facebook & X," and the live HTML links to [Facebook `kenforkansas`](https://www.facebook.com/kenforkansas/) and [X/Twitter `kenrahjes`](https://twitter.com/kenrahjes). The site does not link Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, TikTok, Truth Social, Gab, Gettr, Substack, or Reddit. ([source](https://kenforkansas.com/))

The campaign's July 2025 filing post is the clearest candidate-controlled secretary-of-state message. It says Rahjes filed as a Republican candidate, frames his business-services priority as streamlining filings and improving the One Stop online portal, and frames the elections priority as security initiatives for election officials and poll workers, transparency in election audits, and accurate results. Rahjes is quoted saying, "I will ensure Kansas will be a leader when it comes to election integrity and election security." ([source](https://kenforkansas.com/news/rahjes-files-for-secretary-of-state/))

Public Facebook metadata captured on 2026-05-11 listed "Ken For Kansas. 985 likes; 210 talking about this" and described the page as, "Ken Rahjes is a candidate for Kansas Secretary of State. Currently, he serves as a member of the Kansas House of Representatives for the 110th..." ([source](https://www.facebook.com/kenforkansas/)) The page's current profile image appears tied to the 2025 secretary-of-state campaign refresh. Post-level text and engagement were not reliably visible in logged-out capture.

The X handle exists because it is linked by the campaign site and indexed by Muck Rack. Muck Rack describes the account as Rahjes' personal account and summarizes the bio as, "Small Business Owner, Agriculture Broadcaster, Kansas State representative 110th District (Retweets do not imply endorsement.)." ([source](https://muckrack.com/ken-rahjes)) Direct X post inventory, reposts, follower counts, and post-level engagement were not reliably visible in logged-out capture.
