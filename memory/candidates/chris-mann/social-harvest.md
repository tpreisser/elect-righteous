## Methodology
This harvest prioritizes candidate-controlled official channels first: the campaign website, its RSS feed, social links in the site footer, and platform metadata for those linked accounts. Because Facebook, Instagram, and X limit logged-out timeline access, the harvest also uses credible indexed snippets, public metadata, and news pages that embed or quote candidate posts. Each item above is labeled by capture quality: directly captured official site content, logged-out platform metadata, indexed social preview, allied public post, or credible public coverage.

Engagement counts are point-in-time visible counts captured or indexed on May 11, 2026. They should be treated as minimum visible counts, not complete analytics. Topic counts are a coding of the visible harvested items, not a complete platform export.
