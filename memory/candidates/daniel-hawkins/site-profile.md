# Daniel Hawkins

## Who They Are
Daniel Hawkins is Kansas House Speaker and a 2026 Republican candidate for Insurance Commissioner. He is a Johnson County-based conservative legislator and insurance executive whose public biography emphasizes business experience, legislative leadership, and long-standing ties to Kansas politics.

Hawkins graduated from Emporia State University, served 12 years in the Kansas Army National Guard, and has spent about 30 years in the insurance business. He and his wife Diane married in 1985 and have two daughters and four grandchildren. His campaign site opens with hard-edged conservative branding, which is part of how he introduces himself to voters.

## Their Record
Hawkins has represented the 100th District since 2013 and rose from committee chair to Majority Leader and then Speaker. His public record in this race is tied to both his work in the insurance business and his leadership of the House Republican caucus.

That matters because he is not entering the race as a neutral administrator. He is already a major Republican power broker in Topeka, and his insurance commissioner campaign turns that legislative leadership into a statewide regulatory pitch.
