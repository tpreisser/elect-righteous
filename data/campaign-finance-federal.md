### Compliance Issues

The National Republican Senatorial Committee (NRSC) called on the Senate Select Committee on Ethics to investigate Christy Davis (along with Erik Murray, Sandy Spidel Neumann, and Anne Parelkar) for allegedly failing to submit required financial disclosures within the federal timeframe. Davis's campaign attributed the delay to a change in compliance vendor and stated they are working with federal officials to ensure proper filing.

**Source:** [KSN News](https://www.ksn.com/news/state-regional/kansas-us-senate-candidates-under-scrutiny-over-missing-financial-disclosures/) | [Ballotpedia](https://ballotpedia.org/Christy_Davis)

**Analysis:** Davis is the most prominent Democratic candidate given her USDA background and previous House run. She has existing PAC infrastructure (C00721845) from prior campaigns. Her 2026 Senate fundraising totals have not been publicly confirmed in search results, but given the compliance vendor issue flagged by the NRSC, her filings may be delayed. She acknowledged publicly that "the key is garnering that support, raising enough money early."
