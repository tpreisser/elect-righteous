## VERIFICATION PROTOCOL

For EVERY piece of information found:

1. **Is this the RIGHT person?** Cross-reference: location (Kansas), age, office held, party, photos. "John Smith" in a Google result may not be YOUR John Smith.
2. **Is this CURRENT?** Note the date. A position from 2008 is historical context, not current stance.
3. **Is this from a RELIABLE source?** Official government records > major news outlets > local news > blogs > social media posts > anonymous comments.
4. **Can you get the VERBATIM QUOTE?** Don't paraphrase. Copy exact words.
5. **Do you have the SOURCE URL?** Every fact = a URL. No URL = don't include it.
